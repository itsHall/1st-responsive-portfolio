LIFEPAL

app + minisite

LifePal is a proposed productivity app that offers ways to organize your to-dos, budget, and ideas. There is a minisite to promote the app as well.

This project was split in two: the app and the minisite. To begin, I researched what productivity app I could create. Once I decided, I began making wireframes of the app in mobile, tablet, and desktop versions. After thinking about the wireframes enough, I moved onto mockups to fill in colors and typography. To put the mockups into more context I used InvisionApp.com's prototyping feature service to create a mobile prototype and see the app proposal function.
